🔒 Privacy Policy

Privasa - Private Diary

Effective Date: April 7, 2026 | Last Updated: April 16, 2026

📱 Our Privacy Commitment

Zero Data Collection

This application does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

Zero Network Connection

This application does not request Internet Permission and cannot access the internet, fundamentally eliminating the possibility of data leakage via the network. All features (notes, check-in, albums, to-dos, calendar, file management, etc.) operate entirely offline.

🛡️ Data Storage and Encryption

Local Storage

  • All note content, attachments (photos, videos, audio, files), to-dos, calendar events, and check-in records are stored locally on your device
  • Data is stored in the application's private directory and cannot be accessed by other apps
  • We do not use any cloud servers to store your data
  • The app supports multi-account mode; each account's data is isolated and independently encrypted

End-to-End Encryption

  • All user data is encrypted using the AES-256-GCM algorithm
  • Encryption keys are derived from your password using the PBKDF2 algorithm
  • Encryption keys are stored only in the device's secure storage area (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
  • Session keys are kept in memory only while the app is unlocked and are automatically cleared upon lock

Biometric Authentication

  • You may choose to use fingerprint or facial recognition to quickly unlock the app
  • Biometric data is managed by the operating system; this app only receives the verification result (success/fail)
  • We never access, store, or transmit your raw biometric data

📝 App Features and Data Processing

Notes and Rich Text Editing

You can create diaries, Markdown notes, to-do lists (Todo), and file management records within the app. All content is stored and rendered locally only and is never uploaded to any server.

Album and Media Management

The app supports attaching photos, videos, and audio files to notes or check-in records. All media files are encrypted before being saved to local storage and are decrypted in real time for in-app viewing.

Check-in and Calendar

Check-in records and calendar data are generated and stored entirely locally. The calendar view only reads the creation time of local notes and does not involve any network calendar sync services.

Trash (Soft Delete)

Deleted notes and data are moved to a local trash folder first, where you can restore or permanently delete them. Trash data is also stored with local encryption, and we do not access your deleted content.

📤 Export, Import, and Sharing

Encrypted Export and Backup

  • You can export your data as an encrypted ENC backup file
  • Exported files are encrypted using AES-256-GCM with your current account password
  • Exported files are saved to a location of your choice on your device (e.g., file manager)

Import

  • You can import local encrypted backup files or plain text (.txt / .md) files into the app
  • The import process is completed entirely locally; file contents are never uploaded to the network

Sharing

  • When you choose to share a note or attachment, the app invokes the operating system's native sharing panel (such as iOS Share Sheet / Android system share)
  • Sharing behavior is handled by the OS and the third-party app you select; this app does not directly access the internet to send data

💎 In-App Purchases

The app offers a Pro version upgrade (one-time purchase) through the native in-app purchase systems of the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android).:

  • Purchase transactions are processed by Apple or Google; we do not collect or store any payment information (such as credit card numbers or billing addresses)
  • We only record your unlock status (whether Pro has been purchased) in local secure storage to unlock corresponding features on this device
  • Restore Purchases operations communicate directly with Apple/Google servers; we do not obtain your Apple ID or Google account information

❌ Data We Do Not Collect

  • ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • ❌ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ❌ Location information
  • ❌ Usage statistics or analytics data
  • ❌ Crash logs (unless you actively send them through system channels)
  • ❌ Any network-transmitted data
  • ❌ Payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.)

🌍 Regional Privacy Rights

EU User Rights (GDPR)

  • Right to be informed - Understand data processing through this policy
  • Right of access - Access all your data at any time
  • Right to rectification - Edit and modify your data at any time
  • Right to erasure - Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Right to data portability - Obtain an encrypted copy of your data through the export feature

California User Rights (CCPA)

  • Right to know - We do not collect any personal information
  • Right to delete - Uninstall the app to delete data
  • Right to opt-out - We do not sell any data
  • Right to non-discrimination - All basic features are available for free
